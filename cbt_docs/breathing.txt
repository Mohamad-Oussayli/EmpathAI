Deep Breathing Exercise

This technique helps reduce physical symptoms of stress.

Steps:
1. Sit comfortably and close your eyes.
2. Inhale deeply through your nose for 4 seconds.
3. Hold your breath for 4 seconds.
4. Exhale slowly through your mouth for 6 seconds.
5. Pause for 2 seconds before the next breath.

Repeat this cycle for 2–5 minutes. Focus on the rhythm of your breathing.
